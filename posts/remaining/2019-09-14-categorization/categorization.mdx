---
title: "Categorization"
date: "2019-09-14"
---

There's no need to categorize ourselves. We're often too worried about who we are or in which box do we fit.

That's irrelevant. We should focus on doing our work, whatever it may be, and not on what it's called. As Austin Kleon says: “Do the verb, forget the noun.„

I wish you a great Saturday,

KRISTJAN
