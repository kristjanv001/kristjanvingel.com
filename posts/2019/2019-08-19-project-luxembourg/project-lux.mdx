As some of you may know, I am based in Luxembourg - officially the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg (_Groussherzogtum LÃ«tzebuerg_). This is not the case anymore.

I will be leaving Luxembourg for good in a matter of hours. **The project Lux is complete!** I'll be heading to a more exotic Taiwan now.

I want to share some of the more memorable photos that I shot here during my 6-month stay. Some are good, some are bad, and some are ugly. Some I have shared before, but many of them I haven't shared anywhere until now. Also, I tried to not include the photos already on my portfolio page.

I need to let a lot of these marinate for a longer period of time as I'm not sure about them.

Pro tip: always let your photos marinate for at least a couple of months or even half a year. The longer, the better. This way, you're not emotionally attached to them anymore and can have a more objective view.
