---
title: "If you're not growing, you're dying"
date: "2019-08-28"
---

There is no middle. It can only be one or the other. You're either moving up (upward spiral) or you're going down (downward spiral).

You can see it in many people, they have lost all drive to do anything. There's no spark in their eyes. They are slowly withering away. That's no life at all.
